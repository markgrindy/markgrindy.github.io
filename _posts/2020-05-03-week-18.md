thu-note: Bad idea of the week was taking my cyclocross bike over to the MTB park. 

When I've got lots of biking in my legs, I'm usually OK to huck stuff I have no business hucking. I'll go slow, crash, etc., but it's not yet been painful enough to make me get a mountain bike, even though I'd much rather spin on trails than roads. 

You know those water park slides that twist around like you're spinning down a toilet bowl? Only three rides in the past few weeks, and that's the trail I decided to hop on. It was steep, tight, and had a high bank. Going of 1 m.p.h., I dropped the bike right on my derailleur. 

That snapped the derailleur hanger, which I had already bent pretty badly awhile back. Great fitness luck lately.
fri-note: Failed to replace the deraulleur hanger today. Busy day at work. No appetite for a cross-town trip during rush hour. Even though there are likely way fewer cars on the roads, it wasn't worth it. 

Instead, I made myself a [fizzy margarita](/) and had a nice long zoom date with good old friends from DC. 
sat-note: Picked up a new derailleur hanger today. Huzzah. 
sun-note: After the bike ride, I put on running shorts for the first time in awhile. It was just to lounge around the house in, but still. 

Having these on my legs and having left a lot of sweat on the road, I feel a little bit more like myself.
